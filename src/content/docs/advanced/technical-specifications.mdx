Dawn Web projects are designed to be maintainable, fast, and ready for future improvements. Technical decisions are guided by the goals of the business and the needs of the people using the site.

<Steps>
1. **Content Structure**
Plan pages, collections, media, and calls to action before implementation.

2. **Build Quality**
Keep components reusable, typed where possible, and aligned with the existing design system.

3. **Launch Readiness**
Validate metadata, forms, analytics, deployment settings, and core pages before publishing.
</Steps>

## Integration Techniques

Useful integrations can include booking systems, payments, CRM updates, analytics, email automation, and custom dashboards.
