Welcome to Dawn Web Docs. This guide helps you prepare the practical pieces of a website or digital product project before kickoff.

## What To Prepare

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Business Goals" icon="seti:config">
    Define the action you want visitors to take, such as booking a call, requesting a quote, buying, or joining a waitlist.
    <LinkCard title="Plan your first project" href="/guides/first-project-checklist/" />
  </Card>
  <Card title="Content and Brand Assets" icon="seti:pipeline">
    Gather logos, colors, copy, photos, service details, testimonials, and any required legal or contact information.
    <LinkCard title="Review the checklist" href="/guides/first-project-checklist/" />
  </Card>
  <Card title="Technical Needs" icon="seti:crystal">
    List integrations such as forms, booking tools, payments, analytics, CRM, or email automation.
    <LinkCard title="View technical notes" href="/advanced/technical-specifications/" />
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Project Setup

<Steps>

1. Confirm your main audience, offer, and preferred call to action.

2. Share existing brand assets, content, domain access, and any platform credentials needed for launch.

3. Agree on launch priorities, approval checkpoints, and post-launch support needs.

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip">
  Projects move faster when core content is ready before design begins. Draft copy is fine; unclear goals are what usually slow things down.
</Aside>
