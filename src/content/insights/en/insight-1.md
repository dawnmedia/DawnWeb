Speed is often the first customer experience your brand delivers. If a page feels slow, visitors are more likely to leave before they read your offer, view your work, or make contact.

For small businesses, performance is not just technical polish. It supports search visibility, paid ad results, mobile browsing, and the feeling that your company is reliable.
